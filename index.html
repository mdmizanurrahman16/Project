About Us

As an animal lover, I have seen how hard it is to get quality pet food and accessories in Bangladesh. I remember struggling to find my cat and dog their favourite treats. On one hand, the quality is hugely compromised in available pet foods and on the other they are extremely pricey.I have always wanted to open a pet shop to help out animal lovers like me I started Paw Food so animal lovers like me can get everything they need in one place. Caring for an animal is a kind and loving activity that need not put financial strain on anybody.

That is why I try to offer the cheapest rate for the products in my shop. My shop is well stocked with several brands of quality pet food. To maintain good hygiene and health of your pet, several accessories and products can be used.

I try to offer the best rate at those too, so that every pet owner can put their trust in my shop. My employees are pet lovers like me who try to assist the customers as best as they can.

Together, we encourage the choice of caring for a pet and make sure the animals get the best treatment.
